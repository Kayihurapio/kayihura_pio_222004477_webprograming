About Me

kayihura pio

Hello, I'm Kayihura Pio, a software developer passionate about creating innovative solutions to real-world problems. I graduated from the University of Rwanda (UR) in Huye, where I obtained a degree in Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT).

Throughout my academic journey, I honed my skills in various programming languages and technologies, gaining a strong foundation in software development principles. My curiosity and drive to make a positive impact led me to delve into the world of event management systems.

I am the proud developer of an event management system designed to simplify the process of accessing information about events and shows. This system provides users with a seamless experience, allowing them to effortlessly discover and engage with upcoming events in their area.

With a focus on user experience and functionality, I strive to continuously improve and enhance my software solutions. I am committed to leveraging technology to empower individuals and organizations, making a meaningful difference in their lives.
